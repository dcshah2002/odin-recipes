Macaroni Salad

Description:

This classic macaroni salad is creamy & flavorful. Elbow macaroni and a handful of add-ins in a simple homemade dressing.

Details:

  • Time: 30 Mins [15 Prep + 1515 Cook]
  • Servings:6 Portions

Ingredients:








Instructions:

  1. Cook elbow macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. Combine dressing ingredients in a small bowl.
  3. Add macaroni, dressing, and remaining ingredients to a large bowl.
  4. Mix well to combine and refrigerate at least 1 to 2 hours before serving.
